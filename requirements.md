# SmartRetail AI – Requirements Document
Hackathon: AI for Bharat – AI for Retail Commerce & Marketing Intelligence

---

## 1. Problem Statement

India has over 13 million small retail and kirana stores that operate with little or no data-driven decision making. Most shop owners rely on intuition and past experience to manage inventory, pricing, and marketing.

Key problems faced:
- Frequent stock-outs of fast-selling products
- Overstocking slow-moving or seasonal items
- Heavy wastage of perishable goods
- Lack of demand visibility during festivals and weather changes
- Inability to compete with large e-commerce platforms using AI
- No access to customer behavior analytics
- Inefficient marketing and discount planning

These challenges lead to revenue loss, increased waste, and reduced competitiveness.

---

## 2. Solution Overview

**SmartRetail AI** is an AI-powered assistant that helps small retailers make smarter business decisions through:
- Demand forecasting
- Inventory optimization
- Pricing intelligence
- Customer analytics
- Marketing automation

The solution provides a simple dashboard that converts sales data into actionable insights.

## 4. Key Features & Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Demand Forecasting
System shall:
- Predict product demand for next 7–30 days
- Use historical sales, seasonal trends, weather and festivals
- Provide demand confidence scores

### 4.2 Smart Inventory Recommender
System shall:
- Recommend restocking quantities
- Identify low-stock items
- Flag slow-moving products
- Suggest reorder timing

### 4.3 Dynamic Pricing Suggestions
System shall:
- Suggest price increases for high-demand items
- Suggest discounts for slow-moving items
- Optimize margins and inventory turnover

### 4.4 Customer Segmentation
System shall:
- Group customers into behavioral clusters:
  - Frequent buyers
  - Discount seekers
  - Premium shoppers
- Provide insights for targeted marketing

### 4.5 AI Marketing Assistant
System shall generate:
- WhatsApp/SMS promotional messages
- Festival campaign suggestions
- Discount strategy recommendations

### 4.6 Dead Stock Detection
System shall:
- Identify items not sold within a defined period
- Suggest clearance strategies
- Recommend bundle offers

---

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

- Easy-to-use UI for non-technical users
- Mobile-friendly dashboard
- Fast predictions (<5 seconds)
- Secure data storage
- Low bandwidth usage
- Scalable cloud architecture
